Songs from the Shuttered

A fine-art photographic project dedicated to music

Songs from the Shuttered is a fine-art photographic series co-created over the past decade By Tomer Jacobson and Maxim Golovanov. The project consists of sixteen meticulously staged photographs, each conceived as a visual interpretation of an iconic song.
