About

Created by Tomer Jacobson & Maxim Golovanov.

“Songs from the Shuttered” is a conceptual photographic series that engages with an audacious premise: to translate the affective, narrative, and symbolic content of songs into staged visual photographs.

All images were photographed on location, using live models, and controlled on-location studio lighting.
