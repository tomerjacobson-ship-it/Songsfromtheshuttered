Songs from the Shuttered

A fine-art photographic project responding to songs.

Songs from the Shuttered is a staged photographic series translating songs into still images. Each photograph responds to a specific song, treating music as narrative rather than soundtrack.

The images are constructed through mise-en-scène, live models, and controlled lighting, drawing on cinematic and art-historical references. The project explores how memory and emotion migrate between sound and image.
